Tim and Tom opened Two T's Bar and Grill in 2018.

Previously living in Texas, we wanted to bring the Texas spirit to St. Louis.

Our menu is based on traditional Southern cuisine.

Our atmosphere is dog friendly.

We have a mechanical bull that is free for customers.

We are sports fans and love cheering on the St. Louis Cardinals and St. Louis Blues.

Two T's Bar and Grill supports a wide range of charities through one-off donations, promotional events, and partnerships with charitable organizations.

Reservations accepted.

Offering warm welcomes and friendly service, we treat our guests and staff like family. We look forward to serving you.
