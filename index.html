Welcome to Two T's Bar and Grill

The famous Two T's Bar and Grill is one of St. Louis' top dining destinations.

This steakhouse offers diners a great atmosphere. Our outdoor patio has a great view!

We have all the ingredients for the perfect celebration: an award-winning wine list; a tempting menu featuring prime beef, wild game and seafood.Choose from a variety of vegetables, appetizers, sides, sandwiches, and salads. There is also a buffet option.
